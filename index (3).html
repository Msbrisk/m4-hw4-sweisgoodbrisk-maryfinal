My Favorite Artist: U2

About the Artist

U2 was formed in 1972 when the soon to be drummer, Larry Mullen Jr. put up fliers st his highschool calling for musicians to form a rock band. The first practice for the members that would become U2 was in Larry Mullen Jr.'s kitchen. Bono (Paul Hewsen), the Edge (Dave Evans), Adam Clayton, and Larry Mullen Jr forged a freindship and career that has spanned nearly 50 years. They have won 22 Grammy awards, are in the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and the Kenedy Center Honors. The band has been heavily involved in charitable acts and activism during their career.
